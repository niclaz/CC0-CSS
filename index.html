FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What is Web3Privacy Now?

Web3privacy now is a think-and-do tank aimed at building a culture of privacy industry in web3 by fostering collaboration among privacy advocates and key players, developing tools and sharing knowledge.

Who is the event for?

People interested in privacy topics in the Web3 industry. We aim to make our content accessible to everyone - rather than going into deep technical detail, we'll discuss privacy topics in their broader context.

How do I get to the venue?

The venue is Paralelní Polis - located in the north of Prague near the metro stations Nádraží Holešovice and Vltavská. The address is 475/43, Dělnická, 170 00 Praha 7 (Google Maps) (OpenStreetMap)
